Answering The Curious

What exactly designates an extraction as a "Micro-Lot Reserve"?

Micro-lot beans are sourced from a highly specific, isolated parcel of land on a farm. These lots are harvested by hand and processed completely independently because they exhibit spectacular, unique flavor traits that differentiate them from standard regional blends.

Do you execute custom roasting profiles for private retail allocations?

Yes, we ship globally. To guarantee absolute maximum freshness, we roast to order every Tuesday and dispatch via climate-controlled express premium couriers within 24 hours of roasting.
